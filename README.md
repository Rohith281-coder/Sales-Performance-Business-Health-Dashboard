# Sales Performance & Business Health Dashboard

## Overview

This project is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to help business managers monitor sales performance, profitability, target achievement, customer returns, and operational efficiency. The dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights that support strategic decision-making.

## Objectives

* Analyze overall sales, profit, and order performance.
* Track Year-over-Year (YoY) growth trends.
* Measure sales target achievement across product categories.
* Identify high-performing and underperforming regions.
* Monitor product returns and delivery delays.
* Provide management with key business insights through interactive visualizations.

## Tools & Technologies

* Power BI
* DAX
* Microsoft Excel
* Data Modeling
* Business Intelligence

## Key Features

### Executive Summary

* Total Sales, Profit, Orders, and Profit Margin KPIs
* Year-over-Year Growth Analysis
* Regional Performance Overview
* Category-wise Revenue Contribution

### Target Achievement Analysis

* Sales vs Target Comparison
* Category Performance Tracking
* Achievement Percentage Metrics
* Identification of target gaps

### Operations & Returns Analysis

* Returned Orders Tracking
* Delivery Delay Monitoring
* Ship Mode Performance Analysis
* Operational Risk Identification

## Business Insights

* Generated ₹2.94M in total sales and ₹372.8K in profit.
* Technology category emerged as the highest revenue contributor.
* Standard Class shipping accounted for the majority of delivery delays.
* Identified key regions and product categories driving business growth.
* Enabled faster decision-making through interactive KPI tracking and filtering.
